While waiting to present my master's thesis during the pandemic and lockdown, I decided to explore the world of programming.

I took my first steps in C, but I discovered web development and UX/UI design, which immediately fascinated me.

So, I focused on Design, HTML and CSS; it felt like architecture for the virtual world.

I found a job. However, I wanted more. I wanted to do the whole process. Programming was missing.

To speed up learning, I took a risk and decided to stop working to fully dedicate myself to programming.

I returned to C, this time at a software development school.

Simultaneously, I picked up JavaScript.

Now, I am also exploring React.

The goal is to keep learning more to become better and better!
