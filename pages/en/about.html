Hello!

My name is Rui Peixe and I'm a user experience and user interface designer.

I have studied architecture and urbanism where I had the opportunity to learn the core principles and methodologies of design.

The interest and curiosity for technology got me learning, as a self-taught in my free time, the fundamental programming concepts.

While exploring web development, I have found out a series of related areas that were fundamentally related to experience, organization, psychology, visualization, and interaction.

Essentially, I was discovering UX/UI, and the fact that part of the process was the same as architecture fascinated me immediately.

I started to connect both areas and gradually doing the transition, with the ambition of building a virtual world of digital experiences more functional and simpler, centered on the user!
